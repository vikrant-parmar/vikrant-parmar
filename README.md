### 2. Healthcare Industry
Healthcare applications must comply with standards such as HIPAA, often including appointment scheduling, telemedicine, and patient data management.

A notable example is **Active MediWeb**, **vivovitals**[USA] and etc..., a comprehensive platform that focuses on managing patient records, health indicators, and health profiles. It offers a variety of features designed to enhance individual health tracking and management:

- **Personalized Medical Diaries:** Tailored diaries for individual health tracking.
- **Predefined Diaries:** Ready-made templates for monitoring blood pressure, heart rate, sugar levels, BMI, PEF, and more.
- **Custom Diary Creation:** Users have the flexibility to create their own diaries based on personal health needs.
- **Statistics and Reports:** The platform enables users to generate statistics on observed parameters and print reports.
- **Clear Graphs:** Visual representation of health data for easy analysis and understanding.
- **Emergency Access:** This feature allows authorized personnel to access critical patient information in emergencies, ensuring timely interventions when necessary.

This holistic approach to healthcare management enhances the ability of providers to deliver personalized care while ensuring that patients remain informed about their health status.

**Workflow:**
- Patients browse healthcare providers, book appointments, or consult doctors via video calls.
- Healthcare providers use secure admin panels to manage patient records and appointments.
- Secure authentication, encrypted data transfer, and notifications ensure seamless and confidential communication.

Restaurant table booking systems enable real-time table reservations, significantly enhancing the customer experience. The workflow begins with customers checking availability, booking tables, and even pre-ordering meals. On the restaurant side, bookings are received in real-time, allowing for optimal seating arrangements. The backend management ensures dynamic availability and prevents booking overlaps, making the process smooth for both customers and restaurant staff.

Among the leading platforms in this space is **JustTable**, which offers comprehensive management features for guests, restaurants, and users. Key functionalities include managing logs, handling walk-in and reservation bookings, and providing settings for QR code reservations. Another notable service is **The Dough Thrower** in the UK, which not only facilitates table bookings but also provides various features to streamline restaurant operations. By utilizing these advanced booking systems, restaurants can improve efficiency and deliver a superior dining experience for their customers.

**Workflow:**
- Customers check availability, book tables, and even pre-order meals.
- Restaurants receive bookings in real time to optimize seating.
- The backend manages dynamic availability and booking overlap prevention.
